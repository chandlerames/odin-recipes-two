Chocotaco

Description

We're going to be making one of the finest Taco Bell creations out there. The picture is of the 2022 collaboration with Klondike.

Ingredients

  • Waffle shell
  • Ice Cream
  • Crushed Candy of Choice
  • Chocolate Drizzle Shell (can substitute with Caramel)

Steps

  1. Get that waffle cone nice, warm, and moldable
  2. Fill a nice, even layer of ice cream in the middle
  3. Fashion it into the finest taco shell known to mankind around the ice cream
  4. Sprinkle crushed candy on the top of the taco
  5. Drizzle over with the chocolate shell
