Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna calls for fresh ingredients, including optional add-ins like italian sausage and fire-roasted tomatoes. Using fresh ingredients makes all the difference in the world! After making this lasagna, I had someone tell me it was the best they ever had! You should try making it.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • fire-roasted crushed tomatoes
  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef and Italian Sausage
  2. Boil Noodles in separate pot
  3. Add tomatoes to ground beef and sausage
  4. Add layer of sauce to pan
  5. Add layer of cheese over sauce
  6. Add layer of noodles over sauce and cheese
  7. repeat until full
  8. bake in oven until ready
